Theorem 4.1.7.

Suppose that \(A = [a_{ij}]\) and that \(A \in M_n(\ff)\) with \(n \ge 2\text{.}\) Then \(\det(A)\) can be calculated using cofactor expansion across any row of \(A\text{.}\) In other words, for any \(1 \le i \le n\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} \det(A) = \sum_{j=1}^n a_{ij}C_{ij}\text{.} \end{equation*}
