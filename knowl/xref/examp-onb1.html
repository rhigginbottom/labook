Example 7.2.3.

The following is an orthonormal basis of either \(\rr^2\) or \(\cc^2\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \mcb = \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\[6pt] \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\[6pt] -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \end{bmatrix} \right\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
