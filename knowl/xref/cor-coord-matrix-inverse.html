Corollary 5.5.14.

Let \(V\) and \(W\) be finite-dimensional vector spaces with bases \(\mcb\) and \(\mcc\text{,}\) respectively. Suppose that \(T \in L(V,W)\) and that \(A = [T]_{\mcb,\mcc}\text{.}\) Then \(A\) is invertible if and only if \(T\) is invertible, and in that case, \([T^{-1}]_{\mcc,\mcb} = A^{-1}\text{.}\)
