Theorem 5.5.13.

Let \(U\text{,}\) \(V\text{,}\) and \(W\) be finite-dimensional vector spaces with bases \(\mcb\text{,}\) \(\mcc\text{,}\) and \(\mathcal{D}\text{,}\) respectively. Suppose that \(T \in L(U,V)\) and that \(S \in L(V,W)\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} [ST]_{\mcb, \mathcal{D}} = [S]_{\mcc,\mathcal{D}}[T]_{\mcb,\mcc}\text{.} \end{equation*}
